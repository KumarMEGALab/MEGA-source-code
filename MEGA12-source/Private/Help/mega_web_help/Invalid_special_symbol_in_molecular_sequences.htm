During alignment, gaps must be introduced in sequences that have undergone deletions or insertions. These gaps are known as alignment gaps or indels., and missing data are '.', '-', and '?', respectively.  This error message means that you have attempted to use the same symbols for two or more of these types of sites, or a chosen symbol is not appropriate. For example, do not use N (the ambiguous site symbol for DNA/RNA sequences), or X (the ambiguous site symbol for protein sequences) because they are already available as the IUPAC symbols for molecular sequences.
