Citing MEGA in Publications

If you wish to cite MEGA in your publications, we suggest the following:

(1) When referring to MEGA in the main text of your publication, you may choose a format such as:

MEGA11: Molecular Evolutionary Genetics Analysis version 11 (Tamura, Stecher, and Kumar 2021).

(2) When including a MEGA citation in the Literature Cited/Bibliography section, you may use the following:

Koichiro Tamura, Glen Stecher, and Sudhir Kumar (2021) MEGA11: Molecular Evolutionary Genetics Analysis version 11. Molecular Biology and Evolution 38:3022-3027

(3) If you are using the macOS version of MEGA X please include the following:

Glen Stecher, Koichiro Tamura, and Sudhir Kumar (2020) Molecular Evolutionary Genetics Analysis (MEGA) for macOS. Molecular Biology and Evolution 37:1237-1239

 (Publication PDF available at https://www.megasoftware.net/citations)
