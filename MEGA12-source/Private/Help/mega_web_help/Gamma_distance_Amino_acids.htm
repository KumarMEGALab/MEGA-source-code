Gamma distance (Amino acids)

 

The Gamma distance improves upon the Poisson correction distance by taking care of the inequality of the substitution rates among sites.  For this purpose, you will need to provide the gamma shape parameterRH_Gamma_parameter (a).

 

For estimating the Dayhoff distance, use a = 2.25 (see Nei and Kumar [2000]Nei_and_Kumar_2000, page 21 for details).
