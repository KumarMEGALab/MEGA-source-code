Estimate Rate Variation among Sites (ML)

Rates | Estimate Gamma Parameter According to the gamma distribution, the substitution rate often varies from site to site within a sequence. The shape of this distribution is determined by a value known as the gamma parameter, which is also known as the shape parameter. for Site Rates (ML)

 

This option uses Maximum Likelihood to estimate the amount of variation in the nucleotide or amino acid substitution rates among sites and finds the corresponding alpha parameter for the gamma rate distribution.  Results are given in discrete form.  The parameter value chosen is that which maximizez the probability of the given data.
