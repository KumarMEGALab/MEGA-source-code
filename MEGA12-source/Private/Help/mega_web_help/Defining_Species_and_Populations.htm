Defining Species and Populations

Beginning with MEGA7, it is possible to write command statements in .meg files for grouping taxa A taxon is the individual unit whose evolutionary relationship is being investigated. Depending on the study, “taxa” may refer to species, populations, individuals, or sequences within an individual. into species and populations as well as groups. The syntax for adding species, group, or population specification for a taxon is:

_{group=groupName|species=speciesName|population=populationName}

Examples:

#human_hemoglobin_subunit_alpha_{species=homo_sapiens}

#gag_specimen1_{species=homo_sapiens|population=european}

#gorilla_HBA1_{group=primates}
