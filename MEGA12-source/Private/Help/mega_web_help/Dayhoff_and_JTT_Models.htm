Dayhoff and JTT Models

The PAM and JTT distances correct for multiple substitutions based on the model of amino acid substitution described as substitution-rate matrices. The PAM distance uses the PAM 001 matrix (p. 348 in Dayhoff 1979RH_Dayhoff_1979) and the JTT distance uses the JTT matrix (Jones et al. 1992RH_Jones_et_al_1992).
